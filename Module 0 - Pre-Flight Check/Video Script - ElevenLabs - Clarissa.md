Here's the critical skill to master: When you have a task at hand, how do you break it down into discrete steps? And how do you HAND OFF from one phase to another?

After research, I create a BRIEF. I might even ask the AI: "Can you summarize this entire conversation into a brief to hand off to another AI tool or team member for action?"

Here's a practical example. Let's say you're working on a blog post or a customer email.

Break it down into stages. Research. Strategy. Writing. Brand compliance. Don't ask one AI to do ALL of those in a single chat.

Instead, open a NEW chat for each phase. Set it up correctly. Give it the RIGHT context. Brief it. Then say: "Now do THIS specific task."

If you ask a single AI to research, strategize, write, AND apply brand guidelines all in one shot, you won't get the best solution. Each task deserves its specialist setup.
