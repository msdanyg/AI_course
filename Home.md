## The Transformation Journey

```
SOLO PILOT                          SQUADRON LEADER
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One person, one AI                → Commander of specialized agents
Reactive, one-off prompts         → Systematic Mission Playbooks
Generic, inconsistent outputs     → High-quality, verifiable results
Limited by individual expertise   → Amplified by orchestrated intelligence
```
