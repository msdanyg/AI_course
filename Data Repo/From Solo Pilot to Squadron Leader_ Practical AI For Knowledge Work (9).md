# Internal policies

Our policy allows for PII use with AI. However, the user is responsible for ensuring that PII is only used outside the company based on company policy.

For example:

PII can be uploaded to Claude, gemini, etc.

PII can be used to generate internal reports such as account MEDDIC analysis.

PII can be used to prep a rep for a call with that customer.

PII can be used to draft an email to a specific customer if the rep reviews it and sends it to the contact.

PII can not be used in marketing collateral without the PR department's human review of the material.

In all cases, PII is permitted to be handled by AI.
