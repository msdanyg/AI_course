# Module 11 Flashcards
## Capstone & Governance: Accountability & Certification

---

### Card 1

**Front:**
What are the Three Pillars of AI Governance?

**Back:**
1. **Accountability**: "AI drafts, humans send." You own every output that bears your name. The AI is your collaborator, but you are the author.

2. **Verification**: Apply review standards based on risk. Quick review for internal brainstorming; full review for customer-facing content; expert review for financial/legal documents.

3. **Transparency**: Know what context you provided, how the AI reasoned, and what it cannot do (verify its own claims, access real-time data without tools).

---

### Card 2

**Front:**
What is the Spot-Check Protocol for data analysis?

**Back:**
Don't verify every number—that defeats the purpose. Instead:

1. **Verify methodology** - Did the AI approach the problem correctly?
2. **Spot-check 3-5 data points** - Manually confirm a sample
3. **Confirm logic** - Do conclusions follow from the data?
4. **Test edge cases** - "What about [unusual scenario]?"

If it passes these checks, trust the output. If any fails, investigate further.

### Card 3

**Front:**
What is the "Time-Quality Matrix" for measuring AI ROI?

**Back:**
| | Lower Quality | Same/Higher Quality |
|---|---|---|
| **Faster** | Risky Acceleration | **The Sweet Spot** |
| **Same Speed** | Net Negative | Modest Win |

**The Sweet Spot** = same or better quality in less time. This is where Squadron Leaders operate.

**Net Time Saved** = (Time task would have taken) - (Time with AI + Review time + Fix time)

### Card 4

**Front:**
What are the five phases of the Capstone Project (Squadron Mission)?

**Back:**
1. **Capture**: Voice memo or meeting notes → XML-structured context for Mission Control
2. **Research**: Recon & Radar → Fact Brief with citations
3. **Analysis**: Code execution for data → Documented methodology
4. **Draft**: Structured prompt → One-page strategic brief
5. **Verify**: Human-in-the-Loop → Accuracy, quality, governance, and Red Team checks

All five phases must be demonstrated for certification.

---

### Card 5

**Front:**
What verification standards apply to different output types?

**Back:**
| Output Type | Verification Standard |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Internal brainstorming | Quick review for relevance |
| Draft internal docs | Review for accuracy and tone |
| Customer-facing emails | Full review + fact-check key claims |
| Data analysis | Verify methodology + spot-check calculations |
| Public content | Multi-person review + PR/Legal if needed |
| Financial/legal docs | Expert review + compliance check |

Scale scrutiny with consequence.

---

### Card 6

**Front:**
What is the "Transparency Prompt" to ask before using AI output?

**Back:**
```
Before I use this output, help me understand:
1. What assumptions did you make?
2. What information would change your answer?
3. What are you least confident about?
4. What should I verify independently?
```

This transforms the AI from a black box into a transparent collaborator, helping you identify gaps and risks.

### Card 7

**Front:**
What are the five commitments of the Squadron Leader Pledge?

**Back:**
1. **Accountability**: Owning every output that bears my name
2. **Verification**: Applying appropriate review standards based on risk
3. **Transparency**: Being honest about AI's role when relevant
4. **Continuous learning**: Staying current with AI capabilities
5. **Sharing knowledge**: Helping others develop AI proficiency

*These aren't just principles—they're the foundation of sustainable AI usage.*

---

### Card 8

**Front:**
What does the Monthly ROI Review include?

**Back:**
Every month, reflect on five questions:

1. **Volume**: What percentage of my work involved AI?
2. **Time**: Where did I save the most time?
3. **Quality**: Where did AI most improve my output?
4. **Struggles**: Where did AI add friction instead of reducing it?
5. **Learning**: What new capability should I develop?

*Squadron Leaders who review monthly improve dramatically faster than those who don't.*
