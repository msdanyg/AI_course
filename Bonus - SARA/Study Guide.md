# Bonus Module: SARA
## Quick Reference Study Guide

---

### What SARA Is

SARA (Sales Assistant Response Agent) is a Claude Project pre-configured with ActivTrak's competitive intelligence, positioning guides, battlecards and case studies. It's a Mission-Specific Command Center — walk in and it's ready.

---

### Can Do vs. Can't Do

| SARA Can Do | SARA Cannot Do |
|---|---|
| Competitive positioning | Product documentation |
| Objection handling | QBR deck creation |
| Discovery question generation | CRM integration |
| Outbound messaging alignment | Replace human judgment |
| Case study lookup | Access real-time market data |
| Context reframing | Upload new files (centrally managed) |

---

### Quick-Start Prompts by Role

**AE:**
> I'm evaluating against [competitor]. What are our top 3 positioning angles?

**SDR:**
> Prospect said [objection]. Give me complementary positioning.

**CSM:**
> Customer considering [competitor] at renewal. Draft a positioning brief.

**SE:**
> I need technical differentiators vs. [competitor] for a demo.

**Any Role:**
> Reframe the following through ActivTrak positioning: [paste text]

**First Time:**
> How can you help me? I'm a [your role].

---

### Context Feeding Pattern

1. Copy text from: Granola transcripts, Slack threads, support center articles, email threads
2. Paste into SARA with: "Reframe this through ActivTrak's competitive positioning for [audience]."
3. Review and edit before sending — AI drafts, humans send.

---

### Remember

- Star the SARA Project for quick access
- More context in = sharper output
- SARA is a positioning anchor, not a documentation tool
- Intelligence files are centrally maintained — don't upload your own
- You clear missions for takeoff and own what leaves the runway
